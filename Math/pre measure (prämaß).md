The map $\mu: \mathcal{R} \to [0, \infty]$ with 
- $\mu (\emptyset) = 0$     
- for a finite number of pariwise [[disjunction|disjount]] sets in the ring we have the [[content (inhalt)]] defined a: 
$$\mu (\bigcup_{k =1}^{N} A_k) = \sum_{k=1}^N \mu (A_k)$$
