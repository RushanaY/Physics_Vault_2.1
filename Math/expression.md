It does look a function, that depends on [[free variable]]s 
If we know exacly how many variables we need, then the expression turns into a [[proposition]] and is [[well formed]]
Those kinds of well formed expressions are then called **formulas with free variables** 

This means that a whole expression, like 2+2>1 can be assigned a truth value and we can also use all the powers of [[logical connective]]s to make sense and make combinations

Important, that the expression and the free variable have independant truth values (which is good, because we want to prove something for all possible x for example)
