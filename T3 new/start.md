## classical feld theory 
use the "principle of least action"  and derive the [[Euler Lagrange Equation]], which in classical feld theory with $\mathcal{L}(\phi, \partial_{\mu} \phi)$  instead of $L(q, \dot{q})$ , gives the equations $$\frac{\delta \mathcal{L}}{\delta \phi^a} - \sum_{\mu} \partial_{\mu} (\frac{\delta \mathcal{L}}{\delta({\partial_{\mu} \phi})})=0$$
