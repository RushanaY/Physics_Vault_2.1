Very general notation:
$$\mathbb{R}^n \equiv  \{\vec x = \begin{pmatrix} x^1 \\ x^2 \\ ... \\ x^n \end{pmatrix}| x^1, x^2, ..., x^n \in \mathbb{R} \}$$
For easier notation a [[transpose]] is used: $\vec x = (x^1, x^2, ..., x^n)^T$ 

This also works for the complex version
