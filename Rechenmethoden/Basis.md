It is a [[Linear hull]] with all [[Linear dependancy|linear independent]] vectors. The linear hull has all the vectors that are needed in order to make all the other vectors of the vector space through linear combination.  This is then called **expansion**. 

Every vector space has a basis, as it can be imagined as the "space matter" and is very important in order to use vectors, as those are in their nature dependant on the basis vectors. 

A typical basis is the one of the [[standard vector space]] being defined using the [[Kronecker delta]] $$(\vec e_j)^i = \delta_j^i$$
