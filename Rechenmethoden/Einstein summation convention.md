Main idea is, that we sum up all the elements with the same index: $A_i B^i$

$$A_1B^1 + A_2 B^2 + ...+A_n B^n \equiv \sum_{i=1}^n A_i B^1 \equiv \sum_i A_iB^i \equiv A_i B^i$$
In this simple case of only one index, those are called **dummy indices** or **pairwise repeated indices** and doing the sum is called **contraction** of indices.
