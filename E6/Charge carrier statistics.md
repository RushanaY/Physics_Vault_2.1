
Number of charge carriers in LB:$$n_L (T) = \frac{1}{V} \int_{E_L}^{\infty} D_L (E) f_{FD} (E) dE$$
Number of charge carriers in VB: 
$$p_V (T) = \frac{1}{V} \int_{-\infty}^{E_V} D_V (E) (1 - f_{FD}(E) ) dE$$
