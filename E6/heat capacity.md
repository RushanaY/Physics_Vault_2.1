# [[fermi gas]]

$$C_P = \gamma \cdot T + A \cdot T^3$$
- especially Alkaline metalls fit well with this theory
- problematic are transition metalls because
	- electrons from d orbitals impact the state density a lot, but are also very heavily localised => using the approximation of delocolised ones doesn't make sense 
- Generally some interactions can be ignored: 
	- electron in the crystal potential of positive ions 
	- electrons with phonons (electrons deform grid)
	- electrons with eachother 
	- => those interactions are the reason for [[effective mass]] and impact the mobility of the electrons
