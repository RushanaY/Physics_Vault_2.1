- Recombination rate for [[Fallunterstütze Rekombination]]: $$\frac{d n_{e,h}}{dt} = - k_{trap} n_{e,h}$$
- [[fluoresence]]: (one $e^-$ and one $h^+$)$$\frac{d n_{e,h}}{dt]} = - k_{rad} n_e n_h \approx - k_{rad} n^2_{e,h}$$
- [[Auger Rekombination]]: (two $e^-$ and two $h^+$) $$\frac{d n_{e,h}}{dt} = - k_{aug} n_{e/h} n_{e/h} n_{h/e} \approx - k_{aug} n^2_{e,h}$$
=> total recombination rate $$\frac{ d n_{e,h}}{dt} = - k_{trap} n - k_{rad} n^2 - k_{aiger} n^3$$
