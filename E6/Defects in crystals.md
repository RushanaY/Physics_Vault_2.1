# Number of defects 
Removing one atom from the grid increases the [[internal energy]] by $\Delta U =n \cdot \epsilon$  and the [[entropy]] by: $$S_{kf} = k_b \ln (\frac{N!}{(N-n)!n!})$$
	$N$ is the number of places in the grid 
	$n$ is die number of defected places
