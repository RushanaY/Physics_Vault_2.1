- the idealised, mathematical grid, but not the same as the [[Elemntarzelle (primitive Gitterzelle)]] 
- 3D grid ist made up from the points $$\vec R = n_1 \vec a_1 + n_2 \vec a_2 + n_3 \vec a_3$$
	where these $\vec a_i$ are the **primitive Gittervektoren** (can't be lying in one plane)
	with $|\vec a_i|$ is the [[grid constant]]
- primitive grid cess or [[Elemntarzelle (primitive Gitterzelle)]]
	- has the volume $$V_c = (\vec a_1 \times \vec a_2) \cdot \vec a_3$$

### In 3D 
- [[cubic]] ->  $a = b = c$ and $\alpha = \beta = \gamma = 90^{\circ}$ 
	- [[primitive]]
	- [[fcc structure (flächenzentriert)]]
	- [[bcc structure (raumzentriert)]]
- [[tetragonal]] -> $a = b \neq c$ and $\alpha = \beta = \gamma = 90^{\circ}$ 
	- simple 
	- bcc - raumzentriert 
- [[orthorhombisch]] -> $a \neq b \neq c$ and $\alpha = \beta = \gamma = 90^{\circ}$ 
	- [[primitive]]
	- [[bcc structure (raumzentriert)]]
	- [[fcc structure (flächenzentriert)]]
	- [[basiszentriert]]
- [[monoklin]] -> $a \neq b \neq c$ and $\alpha = \gamma = 90^{\circ}; \space \beta \neq 90^{\circ}$ 
	- [[primitive]]
	- [[basiszentriert]]
- [[triklin]] -> $a \neq b \neq c$ and $\alpha \neq \beta \neq \gamma \neq 90^{\circ}$ 
- [[hexagonal  grid shape]] -> $a \neq b \neq c$ and $\alpha = \beta = 90^{\circ}; \space \gamma = 120^{\circ}$ 
- [[rhomboedrisch]] -> $a = b = c$ and $\alpha = \beta = \gamma \neq 90^{\circ}$
