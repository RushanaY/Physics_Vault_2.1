Calculate [[internal energy]]: $$U_{ij} = \begin{cases} \pm \frac{Q_i E_j}{4 \pi \epsilon_0 r_{ij}} \text{ Coulomb interaction}  \\ \lambda \exp({- \frac{r_{ij}}{\rho}} ) \text{ Pauli repulsion }\end{cases}$$
=> total potential energy of an atom i : $$U_i = \sum_{i \neq j} U_{ij}$$
=> bindung energy of a crystal $$U_{tot} = \frac{N}{2} \cdot U_i = \frac{N}{2} \cdot \sum_{i \neq j} (\lambda \exp (- \frac{r_{ij}}{\rho}) \pm \frac{q^2}{4 \pi \epsilon_0 r_{ij}})$$
