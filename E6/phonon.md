It is possible because of the [[wave particle duality]]. The wave here has particle properties, so therefore is also equipped with :
- momentum $$\vec p = \hbar \vec q$$
- energy $$E = \hbar \omega$$
The oscillation gets exited into the state $n_{\vec q r}$ = creation of a $n_{\vec q r}$ phonon of type $r$ and wave vector $\vec q$ 
==the higher the oscillation, the more phonons of this type exist== 


To get the probability of a phonon being in a position, whe use the [[Bose Einstein distribution]]
