## Assumption 
- solid body is described through N quantenmechanical Oscillators with 3 degrees of freedom 
- they have all the same **frequency** 
- => ==only optical phonons== 

### specific heat capacity in this case 
$$C_V = 3 N k_B T (\frac{\theta_E}{T})^2 \cdot \frac{exp(\frac{\theta_E}{T})}{(exp(\frac{\theta_E}{T}) -1)^2}$$
with approximation at **high** temperatures, where $\frac{\theta_E}{T} \to 0$ we have a relation that corresponds to the [[Duolong Petit law]] $$C_V \approx 3 N k_B$$
with approximation at **low** temperatures, where $\theta_E >> T$ $$C_V \sim exp (- \frac{1}{T})$$
although experimentally $$C_V \sim T^3$$

### Problems 
- at low temperatures optical phonons freeze up 
- -> there is not enough thermal energy to exite them to a higher order 
- -> acoustic phonons dominate 

=> none of that is considerd in the Einstein Modell
