
==it is the mean energy we need in order to add another particle to the system==
==is the amount of energy that we need, to add another electron into the system== 
Denoted with the letter $$\mu$$
- the [[phonon]] gas does not have the same particle number, but [[quasi free electron]] does! (because it is the same, we need the parameter $\mu$)
- it is a [[Thermodynamic]] quantity
	$dU = TdS - pdV + \sum_i \mu_i dn_i$ 
- the relation with [[fermi energy]] is $$\mu (T=0) = E_F$$
- for all other temperatures we have $$\mu \neq E_F$$
	- Temperature dependancy of the chemical potential $$\mu (T) = E_F [1 - \frac{\pi^2}{12} (\frac{T}{T_F})^2]$$
