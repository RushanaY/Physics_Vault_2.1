Then, in that order, they are put at the bottom of their estimated fitting pillar

This serves a similar function to time-marking completed glasses


Update 27.12.2025
During General, Tokens are put into "Completed Tokens" before moving on to the next Token
Before beginning the next General session (different day), these Tokens are evaluated again and put in their estimated fitting pillar
This serves to evaluate how much knowledge stuck from the tokens in the previous session
