# light time 
how long does a photon need to reach :
- moon : 1s 
- sun: 8min
- sirius: 8 years 
- andromeda galaxy: 2.5 Million years 

- light velocity $$c = 3 \cdot 10^8 m/s$$
- allows to look in the past (the bigger the distance the more in the past we look)
- this is the reason why we have a limit to the obervable universe = radius is how far away the first light starting from the big bang got
