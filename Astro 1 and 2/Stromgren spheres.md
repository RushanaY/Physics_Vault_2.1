# Recombination 
- we can get the size of the HII region from equillibrium condition => **ionisation rate = recombination rate** (region gets exited, but doesn't grow)

$$n_{recombination} = \alpha \cdot n_e \cdot n_H$$
	$\alpha$ : recombination coefficient = probability of electron and proton to make a hydrogen atom 
		at $T=800K$ usually $\alpha 3.1 \cdot 10^{-13}cm^2 \space s^{-1}$  
	$n_e$ : electron denstiy 
	$n_H$ : proton density 

# Radius 
$$R_S = (\frac{3 N_{OB}}{4 \pi\alpha})^{1/3} n_H^{-2/3}$$
where $N_{OB} = N_{recomb} = \alpha n_H^2 V$
